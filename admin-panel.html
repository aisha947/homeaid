HomeAid Reviews Admin Panel

Manage customer reviews and testimonials

How to Use This Admin Panel

  • Pending Reviews: New reviews submitted by customers that need your approval
  • Approved Reviews: Reviews that are currently visible on your website
  • Approve: Click the green "Approve" button to make a review visible on your site
  • Delete: Click the red "Delete" button to permanently remove a review
  • Refresh: Click "Refresh Reviews" to load the latest submissions

How the Review System Works

  • Customers submit reviews through your website's review form
  • Reviews are stored locally and sent to you via Netlify Forms
  • You receive email notifications for new reviews
  • Use this panel to approve or delete reviews
  • Only approved reviews appear on your public website

Publishing Approved Reviews

  • After approving reviews here, you need to update your reviews.json file
  • Copy the approved reviews data and paste it into your GitHub repository
  • Netlify will automatically deploy the updated reviews to your live site
  • This ensures reviews are permanently stored and visible to all visitors
